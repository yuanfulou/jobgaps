Interview Artifacts

JobGaps will provide the user with a rich professional resume and cover building tool. Resume and cover letter review will provide you a survey as to how good the quality of your artifacts is. It will compare the user across the pool of similar applicants on the platform to see where they stand. This will help the user to increase his job opportunities by enhancing the resume beforehand!

Applications Tracker

A dashboard that will keep track of all the job applications related processes. This will allow you to view your application date, mode of application, current progress in the application, the deadline for the job you want to apply, your recent applications and when you applied for them. The dashboard will compile all your job applications, job openings and you can view them in one place. Also, it would recommend jobs and internships based on candidates match with the Job description, previous applicants for the same position the previous year and feedback for candidates from the recruiters!
